Hello! I'm Casper Turesson, also known as castur_. I am passionate about developing high-quality, performant software. I specialize in C and C++, but I dabble in a bit of everything (I built this website, for instance). Currently, I'm studying for a Master's in Game Engineering.

I've been developing video games since I was 10 years old. Ever since then I've been teaching myself programming, computer science, maths, game design, art, music, and everything else you need to develop games. I love learning; it's what I do. Nowadays my interest lies in software engineering as a whole, though game dev and graphics programming will always have a special place in my heart. I believe that my wide range of interests enhances my perspective and allows me to approach problem-solving with a more holistic, innovative, and unique mindset.
